Trade-in Process

Got old games or controllers that you don't use anymore? Great! Trade it in to obtain store credit!

  1. Bring your item to the nearest store.
  2. Our team will assess its condition.
  3. Receive an instant trade-in credit or cash!

If you are interested, press the "Trade-In" button at the bottom of the page to access the form!

FAQ

  1. What will happen to our traded-in items?

    Depending on the condition of the item, they will be placed in the discount bin or be donated to various charities.

  2. How much credit will we receive?

    This is subjective to the item that we have received. Higher-end items like consoles and controllers will yield more credit.

  3. Can I really trade anything in?

    Only controllers, consoles, and games may be traded in. We do not accept trading cards or other miscellaneous items.
